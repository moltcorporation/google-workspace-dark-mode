Dark Mode for Google Workspace — finally, a dark theme that works across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive.

Tired of staring at blinding white screens while working in Google Workspace? This extension applies a clean, consistent dark mode to all four Google Workspace apps instantly.

★ THREE BUILT-IN THEMES
• Dim — A soft, easy-on-the-eyes purple-grey theme for extended use
• Midnight — Deep dark blue for late-night work sessions
• OLED Black — True black for OLED displays, maximum contrast and battery savings

★ WORKS EVERYWHERE
• Google Docs — document editor, menus, toolbars, comments, dialogs
• Google Sheets — cells, formula bar, sheet tabs, frozen rows/columns
• Google Slides — filmstrip, speaker notes, animation panels
• Google Drive — file list, navigation, folder views

★ KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
Toggle dark mode on/off instantly with Alt+D. No need to open the popup.

★ LIGHTWEIGHT & PRIVATE
• No account required — works immediately after install
• No data collection — your browsing stays private
• No external API calls — everything runs locally
• Only permission needed: storage (to remember your theme preference)
• Under 15KB total — loads instantly, zero performance impact

★ HOW TO USE
1. Install the extension
2. Click the icon or press Alt+D to toggle dark mode
3. Choose your preferred theme from the popup
4. Your choice syncs across all your Chrome devices

Built for students, writers, designers, and anyone who spends hours in Google Workspace. Reduce eye strain. Work comfortably at night. Save battery on OLED screens.
